About

P large. Current awardees: This page is a launchpad for the Phase II application process in addition to managing your Phase I or II grant.

Seed funding for high-risk R&D with founder-friendly terms.

  • $225,000 / 6 months. Receive up to $225,000 in seed capital towards conducting product R&D over a six-month timeframe (up to 12 months depending on the circumstances).

  • 0% equity. It's non-dilutive funding so you retain ownership of your company. It's not a loan so there's nothing to pay back.

  • Early-stage funding for high-risk technology. While most investors won't back pre-traction companies, we seek to fund nascent, unproven technologies with huge market potential. Then again, most investors don't have access to the National Science Foundation's cadre of experts to conduct technical due diligence.

  • We look for societal impact as much as market potential. Since we don't have the same financial incentives as investors, we're able to make impact investments in technology that may be transformative but may not directly provide a financial return. We look to invest in a better future for our shareholders: American taxpayers.

  • Allocate funds any way you want (almost). Funding may be used for employees’ salaries and wages, fringe benefits, materials and supplies, and other costs related to R&D. Funding may not be applied toward business development or marketing.

  • Standard reporting kept to a minimum. We provide you templates for reporting monthly progress and financials during the award period. No need to waste time crafting decks and building complex spreadsheets.

  • Virtual consultations with program advisors. We encourage businesses to develop from any location — not just in tech hubs. We don't require companies to uproot their team or relocate their business in order to gain access to funding or mentors.
